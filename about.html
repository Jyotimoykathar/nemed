NEMED is a company started today with a Vision for tomorrow to earn a name in Pharmaceutical Industry. Backed by opinion leaders, our management team consists of young and experienced professionals having combined experience of 80 years in Pharma segment.

At NEMED we always believe that great businesses are the results of great partnerships. QUALITY is the hallmark of NEMED. Finest quality has known to be synonymous with our products. Right from the inception stages, quality has found a profound place in all our endeavours. At every phase of manufacturing, right from raw materials to finished products, highest standard of quality is ensured with the help of most modern equipments, trained personals and state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities. Our products are manufactured according to international standards (WHO-CGMP Certified) in selected and audited high-tech facilities. All production facilities match highest standards of hygiene and with all equipment's undergoing periodic inspection from time to time for consistency from batch to batch; the odds of defects are literally zeroed down.
